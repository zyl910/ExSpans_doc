Provides a type-safe and memory-safe representation of a contiguous region of arbitrary memory. It can be regarded as the Span<T> of nint index range (提供任意内存的连续区域的类型安全和内存安全表示形式. 它可以被视为 nint 索引范围的 Span<T>).
